# Open AV Wall 4800

## Requirements

Version: 0.1

---

# 1. Objective

Design an open-source, modular, self-standing AV equipment wall for offices where drilling into existing walls, ceilings, or floors is prohibited.

The system shall support multiple display layouts while remaining easily modifiable using standard aluminum extrusion components.

---

# 2. Functional Requirements

## FR-001

The structure shall be completely self-standing.

No anchors may be installed into the building.

---

## FR-002

Maximum overall width:

**4810 mm**

---

## FR-003

Maximum display configuration:

* 65-inch display ×6
* 3 columns ×2 rows

---

## FR-004

Compatible display sizes

* 43 inch
* 55 inch
* 65 inch

Displays may be mixed.

---

## FR-005

The wall shall support future installation of:

* Cameras
* Microphones
* Speakers
* Lighting
* Mini PCs
* Network devices

without structural modification.

---

## FR-006

Displays shall be mounted using removable VESA modules.

The current design targets the existing VESA wall brackets already owned by the project.

---

## FR-007

The wall shall be movable using casters.

Normal operation shall rely on leveling feet rather than caster wheels.

---

# 3. Non-functional Requirements

## NFR-001

Modular construction.

---

## NFR-002

Manufacturable using commercially available aluminum extrusion.

---

## NFR-003

Designed primarily around MISUMI components.

Equivalent manufacturers may also be used.

---

## NFR-004

All structural members shall be replaceable individually.

---

## NFR-005

The design shall be maintained as open-source hardware.

---

# 4. Design Philosophy

The project is **not** a TV stand.

It is an AV infrastructure platform.

Displays are considered interchangeable modules mounted onto the platform.

---

# 5. Out of Scope

* 75-inch displays
* Building attachment
* Decorative panels
* Hidden aluminum frame

The exposed aluminum frame is intentional.
