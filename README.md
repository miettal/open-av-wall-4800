# Open AV Wall 4800

Self-standing modular AV equipment wall for mounting TVs, cameras, lighting, and cables without drilling into walls, ceiling, or floor.

## Target size

- Width: 4810 mm
- Height: approx. 2400–2500 mm
- Depth: approx. 1500 mm
- Display layout: up to 65-inch × 6, 3 × 2
- Structure: aluminum extrusion frame
- Mounting: modular VESA units
- Installation: free-standing, caster + adjuster feet

## Design policy

- No drilling into building walls, floor, or ceiling
- Modular 3-section structure
- Aluminum frame exposed
- Compatible with mixed display sizes
- Designed for future cameras, lights, microphones, and wiring
